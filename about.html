Company Profile: M/s Gouthami Industries

Pioneers in Precision Manufacturing Since 1991 Founded by T. Konda Reddy in 1991, M/s Gouthami Industries has become a distinguished leader in the precision manufacturing sector, with an unwavering commitment to delivering excellence and innovation. Over three decades, we have carved out a vital role in the aerospace and defense industries, offering cutting-edge, high-quality components for mass production, supported by a robust dedication to stringent quality standards.



Manufacturing Excellence

At M/s Gouthami Industries, we specialize in crafting precision components that meet the rigorous demands of the defense sector. Our adherence to ASTM standards ensures that our products meet global quality expectations. We collaborate with renowned metal and alloy suppliers, both from India and internationally, ensuring traceability back to original manufacturers. For added assurance, materials undergo re-testing at accredited test facilities. Located in Telangana’s industrial hub at 7/9/A, Nadergul, Balapur, Ranga Reddy District, our facility is equipped with advanced infrastructure that supports every stage of the manufacturing process. In-house operations such as machining and assembly guarantee full control over quality, while specialized services like NDT inspections are outsourced to trusted laboratories.


Quality Assurance Commitment

Our process begins with a comprehensive quality assurance plan tailored to each client’s specifications. From material sourcing to inspection checkpoints, we ensure a rigorous and approved plan is in place before any manufacturing activity commences. This commitment to precision is further extended to auxiliary processes, where we maintain a list of approved suppliers for critical applications like hard chrome plating.


Innovative Machinery and Facilities

M/s Gouthami Industries operates with state-of-the-art machinery, offering superior performance and reliability across a wide range of components. Our facility is equipped with CNC Milling Machines (3 & 4 Axis) for complex milling tasks, CNC Turning Machines (2 Axis) for precision turning, and a variety of conventional milling, drilling, and tapping machines that provide unmatched flexibility and capability. Our equipment, including rotary tables, dividing heads, and bench grinding machines, supports intricate operations, ensuring we meet the high standards expected by our clients
