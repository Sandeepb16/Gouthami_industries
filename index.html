About Gouthami Industries

Gouthami Industries, founded in 1991 by T. Konda Reddy, is a leading force in precision manufacturing, specializing in delivering high-quality components for the aerospace and defence sectors. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and advanced production capabilities, we adhere to stringent industry standards such as ASTM. Our state-of-the-art facility, located in Telangana, is equipped with cutting-edge CNC machinery and infrastructure, allowing us to maintain rigorous quality control throughout all stages of manufacturing. Gouthami Industries is dedicated to innovation, precision, and timely delivery, ensuring we meet the demanding needs of our clients worldwide.



Our clients

At Gouthami Industries, we are deeply grateful to our clients who have placed their trust in us and contributed to the growth of our capabilities. Their confidence has been instrumental in our journey toward excellence. Our client list is a testament to the dedication and commitment we bring to every engagement, reflecting our focus on delivering the highest quality and precision in every project.
